Legal Status and Corporate Classification

Sean Pelillo Enterprises operates as a registered sole proprietorship based in Knoxville, Tennessee. This entity serves as the foundational business structure for various digital platforms, web development services, and community-focused software applications, including Gridiron Give. Please be explicitly advised that Sean Pelillo Enterprises is a strictly for-profit commercial business. We are not registered as a 501(c)(3) charitable organization, nor do we hold any special tax-exempt status. Consequently, any application fees paid for digital services, or funds processed through our software platforms on behalf of third-party users, are not eligible for tax deductions as charitable contributions under federal or state law.

Operational Model and Platform Mechanics

Our software solutions are built on an automated marketplace model designed to securely facilitate transactions between community supporters and local organizations. By leveraging enterprise-grade, third-party payment processors, our platforms route funds efficiently and transparently. Sean Pelillo Enterprises sustains its ongoing operations, software maintenance, and hosting infrastructure through transparent application fees applied automatically during these transactions. This modern financial architecture ensures that we can continue providing high-tier, reliable software solutions to local teams and small businesses without burdening them with prohibitive, upfront development costs. We are committed to total financial clarity; users and platform participants will always be informed of any operational platform fees associated with their transactions prior to final submission.

Mission Statement and Local Commitment

Beyond the legal designations and technical architecture, the core of this enterprise is deeply rooted in the local community. As a Knoxville native, I founded this business with a clear, down-to-earth mission: to bridge the gap between dedicated small businesses, youth sports programs, and the modern digital tools they need to thrive in today's environment.

I have seen firsthand the incredible, life-changing impact that well-funded, organized athletic programs have on our youth. However, I have also seen the heavy administrative burdens that coaches, parents, and volunteers face every single season just to keep their programs running. My primary goal is to eliminate that friction. By delivering clean, highly functional software characterized by a straightforward, corporate aesthetic, we remove the unnecessary visual noise and complex interfaces of older systems. This streamlined approach lets community leaders and coaches step away from the screen and focus their energy on what truly matters—mentoring their players and strengthening our local community.
