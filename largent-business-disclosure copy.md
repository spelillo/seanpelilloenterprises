# Largent Business Disclosure

Last updated: June 11, 2026

## Overview
Largent is a personal budgeting and financial tracking application owned and operated by **Sean Pelillo Enterprises**, a registered sole proprietorship based in Knoxville, Tennessee, USA. Largent is a **for-profit commercial software product** designed to help individuals understand monthly take-home income, allocate a realistic ledger, track spending, and optionally connect bank accounts for streamlined transaction review.

## Legal Entity
- **Business name:** Sean Pelillo Enterprises
- **Product name:** Largent
- **Legal structure:** Sole proprietorship
- **Primary jurisdiction:** Knoxville, Tennessee, USA
- **Operating status:** For-profit commercial entity

## What Largent Does
Largent is built to help users:
- estimate monthly take-home income
- build a category-based monthly ledger
- track spending against planned allocations
- optionally connect up to two bank accounts for transaction syncing
- optionally subscribe to premium features for automated budgeting workflows

Largent is a software platform for personal financial organization. It is **not** a bank, credit union, investment adviser, broker-dealer, tax adviser, accounting firm, or law firm.

## Tax and Financial Guidance Disclaimer
Largent provides estimation tools, budgeting workflows, and organizational reporting. Any tax, paycheck, deduction, or budget calculation presented in the product is an estimate for planning purposes only and should not be treated as official tax, payroll, accounting, or legal advice.

Users are responsible for confirming their actual tax obligations, withholding setup, payroll deductions, and financial decisions with appropriate professionals or institutions.
