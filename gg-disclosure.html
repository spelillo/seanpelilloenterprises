2. Financial Transparency & Fee Structure

We believe donors have the right to know exactly where their money is going before they complete a checkout. Gridiron Give utilizes a Direct Payout model. We are not a custodial platform holding your funds in a central pool; donations are routed directly to the recipient's verified bank account.

The 95% Promise

When you support an athlete or team, 95% of your total donation amount goes directly to the recipient.

Platform & Processing Fees Explained

The total deduction applied to a transaction is capped at 5%. Here is exactly how that 5% is utilized:

  • Payment Processing: 2.9% + $0.30 of the transaction is automatically deducted by Stripe, our secure payment gateway partner, to cover credit card and ACH processing costs.
  • Platform Operations: The remainder of the 5% fee is retained by SPE. This covers the costs of server hosting, database security, ongoing software development, and customer support.

Timing & Disputes

Funds are typically available to the coach or player within 2-3 rolling business days, as dictated by standard banking clearing times. Any requests for refunds or disputes are handled in accordance with our terms of service and processed securely via Stripe. Donors can initiate inquiries through our support email.

3. Campaign Verification ("Where the Money Goes")

General fundraising pools often lack visibility. Gridiron Give enforces an itemized accountability standard to close the trust loop between donors and recipients. Every team or player profile is required to maintain:

  • Itemized Needs: Clear targets for specific gear (e.g., "$250 for a new safety helmet" or "$75 for cleats").
  • Real-Time Tracking: Live progress bars showing the exact amount raised versus the remaining target.
  • Fulfillment Updates: Coaches and players are encouraged to post fulfillment updates (e.g., "Helmet purchased!", "Camp fee paid!") to prove funds were utilized as promised.
  • Goal Archives: A historical record of fully funded and completed goals remains visible on the team's profile.

4. Governance, Moderation & Reporting

Maintaining a safe ecosystem for youth sports funding is our highest priority. The platform is actively managed by SPE leadership. We apply strict content moderation standards to all team pages, ensuring imagery, text, and financial goals are appropriate and verified.

Fraud Prevention & Escalation: If a donor suspects a campaign is misrepresenting its goals, or if a team is operating without proper administrative oversight, we provide a direct escalation path. Concerns can be reported immediately to report@gridirongive.org for investigation, which may result in account suspension or fund holds.

5. Bank-Level Security & External Trust

Gridiron Give does not store, view, or process your raw credit card or bank account information on our servers. All financial data is encrypted and vaulted by Stripe.
