Gridiron Give

A specialized fundraising platform built to empower youth athletic teams through modern web tech.

Visit Website

The Vision

Traditional fundraising platforms often feel cluttered and take significant percentages of team revenue. Gridiron Give was designed to be "user-focused, without the extra noise."

The goal was to create a friction-less experience for coaches to launch a campaign in minutes and for donors to support their teams in seconds.

Technical Deep Dive

  • Frontend Architecture: Developed using React for a fast, single-page application experience that remains responsive on mobile devices.
  • Backend & Security: Powered by Firebase. I implemented real-time database listeners to ensure donation bars and goal trackers update instantly across all users.
  • FinTech Integration: Integrated Stripe Connect to handle secure payments and ensure that funds are routed directly and safely to the sports organizations.
