Why Largent

A cleaner way to budget from real monthly income.

Largent is a personal budgeting app built to help people plan month to month with more confidence. Instead of guessing from rough annual numbers, Largent helps users estimate accurate take-home income, map out a practical monthly ledger, and track spending against the plan they actually set.

More accurate starting numbers Income planning begins with realistic take-home pay estimates so budgeting is built on what a user can actually spend each month.
Month-by-month planning Users can allocate money across savings, fixed costs, and variable spending categories in a simple, editable monthly structure.
Real-time transaction visibility Premium bank connections can bring in statement activity so budgets stay aligned with real purchases as they happen.
Built for clarity, not clutter Largent is designed to feel lightweight, understandable, and useful for everyday budgeting without the noise of traditional finance dashboards.
Visit the live Largent app
Key benefits
Helps users budget around true take-home pay instead of inflated gross-income assumptions.
Supports more accurate monthly planning with flexible category allocation and budget tracking.
Can connect to bank statement activity in real time for a more current, practical view of spending.
