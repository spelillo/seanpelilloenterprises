# Largent Plaid Consent Language

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This file contains recommended consent copy for Largent’s bank connection flow.

## 1. Short Consent Copy for UI Near the Connect Button
By connecting your account, you authorize Largent to use Plaid to access selected bank account and transaction data so you can review spending, categorize purchases, and keep your monthly budget up to date.

## 2. Expanded Consent Copy for Modal or Disclosure Panel
When you choose to connect a bank account, Largent uses Plaid to securely access certain financial account information on your behalf. This may include your account name, institution, masked account details, balances if enabled, and transaction history needed to support budgeting and transaction review features.

Largent uses this data only to power your budgeting experience, including:
- identifying new spending activity
- helping you categorize transactions
- updating budget tracking inside your dashboard
- reducing manual entry for premium users

Largent does not use connected account data to make lending decisions, and Largent does not sell your personal financial data.

You can disconnect a linked account later, but previously imported transaction records may remain in your saved budgeting history unless separately deleted where applicable.

## 3. Checkbox Consent Language
I authorize Largent, through Plaid, to access and process my selected account and transaction data for personal budgeting, transaction review, and spending categorization features.

## 4. Premium Bank Sync Disclosure
Bank sync is an optional premium feature. If enabled, Largent may periodically refresh connected account data to identify new transactions and present them for categorization in your dashboard experience.

## 5. Connection Limit Disclosure
Largent currently supports connecting up to two bank accounts per eligible premium account.

## 6. Suggested “Learn More” Language
Largent partners with Plaid to connect your bank accounts securely. Plaid handles the credential connection flow. Largent receives only the data needed to support your budgeting workflow inside the app.

## 7. Suggested Disconnect Language
Disconnecting an account stops future syncing from that connection. Historical transactions already imported into your budget may remain as part of your spending history.
