Business Development Agent

AI-powered quoting and customer intake for local service businesses.

Business Development Agent, also known as BDA, is a software platform that helps local service businesses capture customer requests, ask the right project questions, generate preliminary quotes, and deliver a cleaner quoting experience through a simple website widget.

Owned and operated by Sean Pelillo Enterprises

About BDA

Business Development Agent is designed for service-based businesses that need a better way to handle customer inquiries, estimate requests, and quote preparation. Instead of relying only on phone calls, scattered messages, or generic contact forms, BDA gives each business a structured digital agent that can guide customers through the quote request process.

Each business configures its own services, pricing rules, service areas, operating preferences, quote formatting, and customer communication style. BDA then uses that information to help generate business-specific preliminary quotes and customer summaries.

BDA does not replace the business owner’s final judgment. It is intended to support customer intake and quoting, while the business remains responsible for reviewing and confirming final pricing, service terms, and project scope.

What BDA Helps With

Customer Intake

Guides website visitors through a simple quote request flow and collects useful project details.

Preliminary Quotes

Uses business-specific services, pricing rules, and customer inputs to generate preliminary quote ranges.

Quote Formatting

Creates cleaner quote summaries and quote documents using the business’s selected quote format.

Website Widget

Allows businesses to place a Business Development Agent directly on their website.

Business Rules

Supports company-specific service areas, policies, pricing assumptions, and estimating standards.

Lead Summaries

Helps organize customer requests so businesses can respond with better context and less manual work.

How It Works

1. Set up the business profile Add company information, service areas, operating details, and contact information.
2. Add services and pricing rules Configure services offered, base rates, minimum charges, quote assumptions, and policy details.
3. Test the agent Run sample customer scenarios to review how the agent asks questions and prepares quote responses.
4. Deploy the website widget Add the BDA widget to the business website so customers can request quotes directly.

AI-Assisted, Not Fully Automated Decision Making

BDA uses AI to assist with customer intake, quote preparation, response drafting, and quote summaries. Generated quotes are preliminary estimates based on the information available at the time of request.

Final pricing may change after inspection, measurement, material confirmation, scheduling review, or changes to project scope. Businesses are responsible for reviewing quotes before treating them as final.

Data Handling Overview

BDA collects account information, business profile information, service and pricing rules, quote settings, customer quote request details, and subscription status information needed to operate the platform.

BDA does not currently rely on business document uploads for onboarding. Business information is entered through structured forms, quote settings, policy fields, and agent preference setup.

Payment processing is handled through Stripe. Authentication may be handled through Replit Auth, Clerk, or another authentication provider used by the platform.
